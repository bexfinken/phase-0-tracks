Vultures!

They are amazing. If we didn't have vultures, what would the world be like?

  • There would be rotting carcasses all over the place
  • We would have way more diseases
  • There wouldn't be any detective vultures in Germany
  • There wouldn't be any adorable baby vultures

So, what are the best things about vultures?

  1. Vomit is their weapon of choice
  2. Vultures are the Benicio DelToro of Birds
  3. Did I mention baby vultures?

Next time you see a vulture--and you will, unless you're in Antarctica or Australia--don't hate. Appreciate.
